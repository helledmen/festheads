Fests From All Over
Waha Festival
A very chill place, in the middle of the forest.People from all over the world come to enjoy the peace that this festival has to offer. Nevertheless if you are looking for more than peace you can also have it all here.
Waha Official

Electric Castle
The most beloved annual music event in Romania, Electric Castle is a unique and genuine 5 days festival experience that happens every year at the Bánffy Castle domain in Bonțida - a tiny historic village near Cluj Napoca in Transylvania.
Electric Castle Official

Neversea
Neversea is a electronic and EDM music festival on Constanta beach in Romania. Curated by the same team behind the legendary Untold, festival-goers can expect the same superstar lineup and high-end production associated with the Cluj-Napoca festival.
